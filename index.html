A brief history

My first job out of college was as a black box software QA Engineer for Macromedia. This meant I wrote absolutely zero code, tracked automated test suite results and wrote bug reports (which I was very good at).

Eventually I found my way into the career I dreamed of, Visual FX. I progressed up from 3D generalist, to 2D Compositor on films, tv and games [imdb page] and finally held the position of Motion Capture Supervisor at studios in San Francisco and San Diego. After almost a decade of work in the biz, 70-90+ hour work weeks for 6 month stretches began to take their toll and I decided I needed a career change.

Two years ago I began learning C++ and I've recently completed the lower division courses along with intro to Java at Portland State University. A fellow student mentioned web dev schools and online courses and then it was, as they say, 'on'...
